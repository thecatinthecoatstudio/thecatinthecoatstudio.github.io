# Privacy Policy

**StageLyrics**

**Last updated:** July 1, 2026

---

## Overview

Thank you for using **StageLyrics**.

StageLyrics is developed with an **offline-first** philosophy. Your songs, settings, and other data remain on your device whenever possible.

We believe your personal information belongs to you.

---

# Information We Collect

StageLyrics does **not** require user registration or sign-in.

The application does **not** collect, transmit, sell or share your personal information.

We do **not** collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Microphone recordings
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics data
- Usage statistics
- Device tracking information

---

# Local Storage

StageLyrics stores the following information **only on your device**:

- Songs
- Collections
- Setlists
- Application settings
- Backups created by you

This information is never uploaded to our servers because StageLyrics does not use cloud storage.

---

# Imported Files

When you import text files into StageLyrics, those files are processed locally on your device.

No imported files are uploaded or transmitted.

---

# Internet Connection

StageLyrics is designed to work without an Internet connection.

The application does not require online accounts or cloud synchronization.

---

# Permissions

StageLyrics requests only the permissions required for its core functionality.

Any access to files is initiated explicitly by the user through the Android file picker.

The application does not access your files without your direct action.

---

# Third-Party Services

StageLyrics does not use:

- Advertising services
- Analytics services
- User tracking services

At the time of writing this Privacy Policy, StageLyrics also does not use crash reporting services.

---

# Data Sharing

StageLyrics does not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties.

---

# Children's Privacy

StageLyrics does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

# Data Security

Since your information remains on your own device, you remain in control of your data.

You are responsible for protecting your device and any backup files you export.

---

# Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
